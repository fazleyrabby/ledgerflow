# 1. Executive Summary

LedgerFlow is a premium finance platform that enables businesses to
manage invoices, payments, analytics, reporting, budgeting, cash flow,
and team collaboration from a single modern dashboard.

The objective of this project is to design and build a world-class SaaS
landing page that communicates trust, performance, simplicity, and
enterprise readiness while maximizing visitor conversion.

The experience should feel comparable to products from:

-   Stripe
-   Linear
-   Mercury
-   Ramp
-   Vercel
-   Arc
-   Apple
-   Notion

The design should avoid generic SaaS templates and instead emphasize
premium visual hierarchy, polished interactions, excellent typography,
generous spacing, and high-quality custom UI mockups.

# 5. Design Principles

-   Prioritize clarity over decoration.
-   Every section should have a clear visual hierarchy.
-   Motion should reinforce usability rather than distract.
-   Use whitespace generously.
-   Maintain consistency through design tokens.
-   Prefer asymmetrical layouts when appropriate.
-   Every illustration should support the content.
-   Avoid stock photography.
-   Use realistic product UI wherever possible.
